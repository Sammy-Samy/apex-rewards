# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in Apex Rewards a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity.

## Standards

Positive behaviour: welcoming language, respecting differing viewpoints, gracefully accepting constructive criticism.

Unacceptable behaviour: harassment, trolling, publishing others' private information, or any conduct reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Report violations to **conduct@apexrewards.app**. All complaints will be reviewed promptly and confidentially.
